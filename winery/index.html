Winery Passport

is the wine industry's most popular mobile app for winery tourism. Our audience of qualified consumers don't just have an affinity for wine, they have a passion for going to where it's made. Winery Passport helps wineries build brand awareness and customer relationships in the mobile space, where more time is spent than desktop. This leads to improved direct-to-consumer revenue.

Benefits

Brand Awareness:

Recommended wineries recieve increased exposure above 6,000+ other wineries in Winery Passport. This ensures your winery isn’t lost among the many options. Whether our audience is searching nearby, on a map, by region/state/province or name, your winery will always appear at the top of the list with a distinguishing badge.

Customer Relationships:

Every stamp in Winery Passport opens a direct line of communication between the winery and that consumer. While only 23% of tasters ever leave an email address, we have contact info for 100% of those who stamp. That means you can send an email/push/in-app message to every consumer who records a visit at your winery, or even has it on their wish list. This audience also has the ability to send your winery tasting, club and purchase leads directly from Winery Passport.

Qualified Audiences:

The Winery Passport audience is not incentivized to visit your winery through offers and promotions, although you’re more than welcome to include them in your listing. That means our audience has an affinity for great wine tasting experiences, which makes them more qualified, and likely, to buy wine or join your wine club.

Mobile:

Mobile represents 65% of all digital time spent — that's two out of every three minutes, according to ComScore. 90% of that time is spent in apps. Consumers are no longer tied to their desktops. In fact, 1 in 5 millennials never touch a desktop computer. Why is this all important? A Wine Institute survey showed 90% of wine drinkers now own a smartphone.

Faq

- How do I stamp someone's passport?

Your tasting room staff don't have to do a thing. A Winery Passport user can stamp their Passport while they're at your winery or not. If the user does have their device's location services turned on we'll record the stamp as having taken place there.

- My time is limited. How much work does Winery Passport require?

Winery Passport will take up as little time as you'd like. Once you set up your listing (which we are happy to take care of for you) you don't have to do another thing. All wine tasting, purchase and club leads will be sent to your email. You'll only need to log in to your custom dashboard to send messages and see performance data.
