The Elen Sweater Company was founded in 1993, and has been a supplier of high quality sweaters for fashion brands for three decades. Our offices and showrooms are located in the city of Guangzhou. We have hundreds of our latest samples on display in our attractive showrooms, and our staff are always prepared to host clients who wish to examine our products. Our factory in Shantou City has over 1,000 square meters of machinery, with more than 300 skilled workers from China's garment industry.

THE HIGHEST QUALITY GOODS AT GLOBALLY COMPETITIVE PRICES

The Elen Sweater Company prides itself on three decades of reliable service as a competitive supplier of high quality sweaters to fashion chains from around the world. We have developed long-term partnerships with clients from Europe, America, the Middle East and South America. We like our clients think of us as their partners in China, bringing them all the latest options in high quality sweaters at the lowest possible prices. We strive daily to maintain our reputation for our standards of business professionalism, client service and product quality.

OUR
FACTORY

Our Shantou City factory can meet the needs of any global fashion chain. The Elen Sweater Company can fill orders for every type of sweater, including 12G, 7G, 5G and 3G.
